While "BCD arithmetic, Part 1" aimed more at the innards
of the NMOS 6502, this article discusses/dissects
the concepts used in old ALU/adder ICs.
...Not to mention the Wang 700 calculator.

This article doesn't cover all the basics,
because I assume that you already did
read BCD arithmetic, part 1.
