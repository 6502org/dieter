Fun with majority gates.

In 2009, I did meet Kio, another electronics
hobbyist, and we had a little talk about how
to build majority gates, and how to use them.

I want to thank him for sharing his ideas.

Didn't pay much attention to majority gates
before this, but because the practical use of
such gates suddenly became obvious to me,
I decided to take a closer look.

For more info about how to use majority gates,
please consult/dissect publications about
quantum dot cellular automata.

This article might be interesting to read
for those who fail to buy exotic ICs...
like 7410.
