The 74882

The somewhat exotic 74AS882 from TI was designed for linking
8 ALUs together... but it only had 4 carry outputs.

Makes sense, because it reduces the circuitry inside
the carry chain, and the wiring between the ICs looks
a little bit nicer.

I wish, I would have used a similar concept in my MT15 CPU.
